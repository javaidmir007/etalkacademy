PRIVACY POLICY

Your Privacy Matters to Us

At ETalk English Academy, your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website and services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, and other details provided through our contact forms or course registration.
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent, and technical data like browser type, IP address, and device information.
  • Communication Data: Messages you send through our forms, chats, or email.
2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and provide support
  • Process course registrations and send confirmations
  • Improve our services and website experience
  • Send updates, newsletters, and important announcements (you can opt out anytime)
3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal data. We may share your data only with trusted third parties who assist us in operating our website or delivering services—under strict confidentiality agreements.

4. Data Security

We implement strong security measures to protect your information. While we strive to keep your data safe, no method of online transmission or storage is 100% secure.

5. Cookies

Our website may use cookies to enhance your experience. You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings, though some features may not function properly.

6. Third-Party Links

Our site may contain links to external websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites. Please review their privacy policies before sharing any data.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request corrections or deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent or object to data processing

To make a request, email us at privacy@etalkacademy.com.

8. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. All updates will be posted on this page with a new effective date.
